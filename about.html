About Us

HR Management System

This project focuses on developing a web-based Human Resource (HR) management system intended to streamline important HR procedures like managing excuses, leave requests, and employee onboarding. The manual and dispersed nature of traditional HR processes results in errors, inefficiencies, and a bad employee experience. Through the use of automation and contemporary online technology, this system will consolidate HR operations into a single, easily navigable platform.
